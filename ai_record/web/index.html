Before you record

AI Record captures microphone and/or system audio locally to produce a transcript. Please read and understand the following before recording.

  • Recording other people without their knowledge or consent may be illegal where you live. Many jurisdictions have two-party (all-party) consent laws.
  • This app captures audio through your system's loopback device. As a technical consequence of that method, the meeting platform does not display a recording indicator. This is a side effect of how loopback capture works, not a feature for concealing your recording.
  • You are solely responsible for obtaining any consent required and for complying with the laws and policies that apply to you.

You can reopen this text anytime from Settings → Legal & Consent.
